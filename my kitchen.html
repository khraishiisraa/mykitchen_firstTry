Dolma

Ingredients:

  • 20 eggplants
  • 20 peppers
  • Grape leaves
  • 400g minced meat
  • 2 cups rice
  • 3 onions
  • 4–5 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp pepper paste
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 bowl tomato sauce
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • ½ cup pomegranate molasses
  • Parsley pinch
  • Black pepper, paprika, salt
  • Sumac
  • 5–6 ribs

Instructions:

  • Boil grape leaves 10 minutes.
  • Hollow vegetables.
  • Mix filling well.
  • Stuff vegetables.
  • Arrange in pot with ribs.
  • Cook 45 minutes.

Soft Multi-purpose Dough

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp yeast
  • 5 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions:

  • Mix all ingredients.
  • Let rest 1 hour.
